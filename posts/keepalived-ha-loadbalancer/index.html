Meet keepalived - High Availability and Load Balancing in One

Posted Dec 11, 2021 Updated Feb 22, 2024
By Techno Tim 2 min read

In my quest to make my services highly available I decided to use keepalived.keepalived is a framework for both load balancing and high availability that implements VRRP.This is a protocol that you see on some routers and has been implemented in keepalived. It creates a Virtual IP (or VIP, or floating IP) that acts as a gateway to route traffic to all participating hosts.This VIP that can provide a high availability setup and fail over to another host in the event that one is down.
